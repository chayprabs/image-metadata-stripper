## Metadata removal

ExifScrub attempts to remove or report metadata using automated tools. **No tool can guarantee** removal of all hidden, proprietary, steganographic, or future metadata formats. Outputs must be independently verified before publishing sensitive material or relying on them for legal, regulatory, forensic, or security purposes.

## Prove-clean attestations

JSON and PDF "prove-clean" reports are **informational technical summaries** generated at processing time. They are **not**:

- Legal certificates or court-ready evidence  
- GDPR/CCPA/HIPAA compliance guarantees  
- Warranties that a file is anonymous or safe to share  
- Substitutes for professional legal or security review  

Cryptographic signatures demonstrate integrity of the report payload as generated by the tool; they do not certify truth of metadata claims beyond what the tool detected.
